Who I am

A builder at heart, an optimist by choice.

I grew up endlessly curious about how things work — taking ideas apart and putting them back together better. That curiosity carried me to IIT Jodhpur, where I earned my M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning — and then somewhere even more interesting: building businesses.

What drives me isn't technology for its own sake. It's the moment a thing I've built actually helps someone — a team that finds answers in minutes instead of days, a business that serves its customers better than it could before. I've spent my career chasing that moment, from my early days building financial technology to building AI systems that enterprises rely on.

Today, as cofounder of rhobots.ai, I work with enterprises to make AI genuinely useful — not a buzzword, but a dependable part of how they operate.

Beyond work, I believe in keeping life simple: stay curious, stay humble, take care of the people around you, and do work you're proud of.

The journey so far

Every chapter taught me something new.

  1. The foundation

    Progcap · Financial technology

    I started my career helping build technology that brought credit and financial tools to small businesses across India — and learned what it takes to ship products that people genuinely depend on.

  2. Going deeper

    IIT Jodhpur · M.Tech, AI & Machine Learning

    As artificial intelligence began reshaping every industry, I went all in — earning my M.Tech in AI & Machine Learning from IIT Jodhpur while designing and shipping AI systems for real-world use, and learning what separates a flashy demo from something a business can truly depend on.

  3. The mission today

    Cofounder · rhobots.ai

    Now I'm focused on one of the most exciting shifts of our generation — helping enterprises put AI to work in the real world, where reliability matters more than hype.
